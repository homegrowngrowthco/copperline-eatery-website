Family Owned Since 1993

Serving Western Massachusetts for more than 33 years!

Since 1993, Copperline Eatery has served the Chicopee & Western MA community. Whether you're one of our 'regulars' or thinking about visiting us for the first time, we hope to see you soon!

Every meal is prepared from scratch, using the freshest ingredients and time-tested recipes from over 30 years in business and over 150 years of combined restaurant experience. We're known for a few signature dishes, most notably our homemade corned beef hash and signature hollandaise sauce over eggs benedict. Whatever you're in the mood for, we're confident you'll find something you'll enjoy.

Thank you for supporting our small, family-owned restaurant and letting us be a part of your community. Whether you're dining with friends, ordering takeout for family, or catering a special event, we look forward to serving you with great taste for many years to come.
